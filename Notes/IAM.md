CIA - Confidentiality , availability and integrity. 
Non repudiation- Not refusing to recognize the validity of something. 
DAD-Disclosure of data, alteration of data and destruction/denial of data. 
Security Models : Bell-LaPadula Model(write up read down), The Biba Integrity Model( read up write down) , The Clark-Wilson Model.
Trust but verify : This requires automated security mechanisms, such as proxy, intrusion detection, and intrusion prevention systems.
Zero trust - authenticationn and authorization. 
ISO/ICE 19249 - Domain separation, Layering , encapsulation , redudancy and virtualization. Design principles : least privilige , attack surface minimization, centralized parameter validation, centralized general security services, preparation for error and exception handling. 
IAAA- identification, authentication, authorization and accountibility are the 4 pillars of Information Security.
**Identification** : Username, email address , unique ID number etc
**Authenticationn** : prove their Identity. Example : entering right password. 
**Authorisation** : determines what the user is allowed to access. 
**Accountability** : tracks user activity to ensure they are responsible for their actions.
